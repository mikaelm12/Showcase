Super Man

Bio

I am an artist from the planet Krypton. My main source of inspiration is the sun and kicking ass, the main object of my artwork.

Email

super.man@dc-comics.com

Super punch
I painted this from memory. This was the day I punched Lex Luthor so hard he lost all his hair.

The sun
I made this oil canvas painting with just three colors: red, yellow and black. Black represents the black hole I once punched out of the milky way to save earth. Red reminds me of 'Sol' the red giant around which my home planet Krypton revolves. And yellow is my favorite color because I love ducklings.

Rage
